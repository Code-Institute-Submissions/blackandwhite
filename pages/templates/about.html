About

The birth of the Coffeeshop Company can be traced back to 1971 when the first Black & White opened its doors to its guests on a historical September day in Bournemouth. Characterized by a very special and unimitable atmosphere, the Black & White is a new, unique interpretation of the ever-popular traditional.
